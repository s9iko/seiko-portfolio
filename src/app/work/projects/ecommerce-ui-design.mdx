## Overview

SneakHaven is a sneaker resell UI mockup covering the full e-commerce flow—browse, product detail, cart/checkout, account, and brand story—designed end-to-end in Adobe Illustrator.

## Highlights
- Hero, collections, and collab features to spotlight drops
- Product grid + detail page with gallery, reviews, and size selection
- Cart, checkout, and order history for a complete shopping journey
- Login/account plus brand story pages to round out the experience

## Design Notes
- Clean, product-first layout with organized navigation and clear filtering
- Consistent SneakHaven branding across every screen

## Tools
- Adobe Illustrator

## Outcome
Delivered a cohesive UI kit for a sneaker marketplace, showcasing a complete buyer journey and brand story in one mockup set.
